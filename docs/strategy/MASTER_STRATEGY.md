## [SHIELD] RISKS & MITIGATION

### Technical Risks:

**1. IPFS Scaling Issues**
- Mitigation: Aggressive caching, CDN layer, IPFS alternatives

**2. Name Squatting**
- Mitigation: PoW, DNS verification, community challenges

**3. Malicious Content**
- Mitigation: Client-side filtering, node operator choice, reporting

### Market Risks:

**1. "Too Technical"**
- Mitigation: Extreme UX focus, templates, one-click tools

**2. "Chicken and Egg" (no users, no nodes)**
- Mitigation: You run initial nodes, make running nodes EASY

**3. "Platform Backlash"**
- Mitigation: Can't be stopped, decentralized, no company to sue

### Competition:

**1. Existing Protocols (IPFS, Dat, etc.)**
- Advantage: FRW is simpler, focused on publishing

**2. Web 3.0 / Blockchain Projects**
- Advantage: No tokens, no hype, just works

**3. Traditional Platforms Improve**
- Advantage: They CAN'T decentralize without losing control
